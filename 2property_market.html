Property Market Insights

Insights:


  • The property with the highest number of sales was in the Queens location (29 thousand units) while the fewest was in the Staten Island location (7.5 thousand units), with a difference in total sales of 38.6%.
  • The borough with the most expensive average sales price is Manhattan (18.6 million), while the borough with the cheapest average sales price is Staten Island (530.9 thousand).
  • Manhattan is the borough with the highest average sales price, but is not among the areas with the highest number of sales.
  • The building category with the highest sales price is Condo Coops with an average sales price of 19.7 million.


Recommendations:


  • Continue and enhance the existing promotion in Queens.
  • Analyze purchase preferences and sales volume in Queens and Staten Island to develop an appropriate strategy.
  • Adjust marketing and sales strategies in Manhattan with a focus on the luxury property market.
  • Although the average sale price is lower, properties in Staten Island have potential as long-term investments due to promising property value growth.
  • Increase production and development of Condo Coops as the most profitable segment.
  • Find strategic locations and carry out renovations to increase property value.
